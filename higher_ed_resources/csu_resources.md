# California State University and Community College Resources
Information on students’ rights, campus policies/ protocols, and existing college-affiliated resources for students on the Cal State campuses and at various community colleges in Los Angeles.

- Free immigration [legal services](https://findyourally.com/) for Cal State and community college 
students.
- Los Rios Community College District (LRCCD)
    - [How to respond to Immigration Enforcement on campus](https://losrios.edu/student-resources/support-services/undocumented-student-resources/how-to-respond-to-immigration-enforcement-on-campus). 
    - Contact Los Rios Police Department at (916) 558-2221. 
    - Free Legal [Resources and Case Support](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1DvlHq0yDElrgVidbg_aq0sHzK1At0dUlbBiTZNESJ8c/edit?tab=t.0) for Students, Staff, and Faculty.
    - Copy of LRCFT [email](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1v88dxzfrTfrcC0bUmj2ahD6vIRub-ATK/view) to Faculty on Responsibilities in the Event of an ICE Raid.
