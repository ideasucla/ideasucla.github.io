# Healthcare and Wellness
These links offer information, support, and other organizations and resources geared towards helping folks protect their physical, mental, and emotional well-being amidst the stressors of today’s immigration landscape.

- [Beyond DACA Directory](https://www.higheredimmigrationportal.org/effective_practice/beyond-daca-a-directory-of-resources-for-undocumented-students-individuals/): comprehensive list of resources, organizations, and mental health providers. 
- Informed [Immigrant’s Mental Health for Immigrants guide](https://www.informedimmigrant.com/resources/healthcare/mental-health-undocumented-immigrants/) provides tips for managing your well-being and supporting others. 
- [ImmigrantsRIsing: Mental Health resources](https://immigrantsrising.org/mental-health/?utm_source=Immigrants+Risi).
- [Developing Psychotherapy Strategies to Foster Resilience and Prevent Trauma Among Immigrants - United We Dream](https://unitedwedream.org/resources/developing-psychotherapy-strategies-to-foster-resilience-and-prevent-trauma-among-immigrants/).
- [UndocuBlack’s Mental Wellness Initiative](https://undocublack.org/mentalwellnessinitiative).
- [Family Separation in the Sunshine State](https://famsepfl.org/): Tools for those suffering family separation. Eleven “Action Steps” to reduce the effects of family separation such as the utilization of a coding system for these cases, establishing community liaisons, creating MOUs with foreign consulates and communication plans with parents. These are some of the key elements to achieving the foster care mission of family reunification. 
- [Medical Expansion Resource Hub](https://caimmigrant.org/what-we-do/policy/health-public-benefits/medi-cal-expansion-resource-hub/): Information needed to access healthcare benefits in California, regardless of immigration status. Provided by the California Immigrant Policy Center.
- [Mental Health Connector](https://immigrantsrising.org/mental-health-connector/): The Immigrants Rising program connects undocumented students in California with licensed therapists, free of cost.
- [Wellness Support Groups for Students](https://immigrantsrising.org/supportgroups/): Wellness support groups hosted by Immigrants Rising, with upcoming groups in June 2026.
- [CAPS @ UCLA](https://caimmigrant.org/what-we-do/policy/health-public-benefits/medi-cal-expansion-resource-hub/): Counseling and psychological services at the University of California, Los Angeles.
