# Know Your Rights (KYR) Resources for the Undocumented Community
Use these resources to learn about the rights that immigrants in the United States possess. These rights outline the conditions under which you (or anyone else) do or DO NOT have to comply with ICE. These resources are intended to help folks exercise their rights and defend themselves in case of a confrontation with ICE or other officials. Red Cards/Tarjetas Rojas are downloadable/ printable cards that list out the rights you can exercise to defend yourself from ICE. They come in many different languages and are usually double-sided, with one side in English and the other in your preferred language. You can hand these cards to ICE officers for them to read as a way to  exercise your rights, protect yourself, and not engage with ICE.
