# Legal Resources for the Undocumented Community
These websites and databases can help folks, either in California or across the country, find the legal services or immigration attorney they need. These resources also offer information on how to avoid fraud when searching for these services.

- California-Based Services
    - [Find Your Ally](https://findyourally.com/) Free immigration legal support for students, staff, and faculty at CCC.
    - [UC Immigrant Legal Services Center](https://ucimm.law.ucdavis.edu/): Support for undocumented students and families across the UC system.
    - CDSS [Immigration Legal Services Contractors](https://www.cdss.ca.gov/benefits-services/more-services/immigration-services/immigration-legal-service-providers): These nonprofit organizations provide consultations and direct legal services to immigrants who reside in the state of California.
- National Directories and Guides
    - Informed Immigrant’s How to Find an Immigration Lawyer [Guide](https://www.informedimmigrant.com/resources/legal-help/how-to-find-an-immigration-lawyer/) - contains information on how to find an immigration lawyer, how to prepare to meet with a lawyer, how to avoid fraud, and more.
    - The National Immigration Legal Services [Directory](https://www.immigrationadvocates.org/nonprofit/legaldirectory/) allows users to search for free or low-cost immigration legal service providers by state, county, or detention facility.
