# Guide for Select Occupations
These resources are tailored for folks in 
particular professions, industries, or roles which have specific guidelines for how to deal with ICE. Use these resources to know what rights and protections folks 
providing and receiving the services in these industries or situations have when faced with ICE.

- **Educators**: [Frequently Asked Questions](https://unidosus.org/wp-content/uploads/2021/07/firm_resourcesforeducators.pdf) For School Officials: What To Do If Ice Shows Up At Your School 
- **Healthcare Professionals** [Know Your Rights, Know Your Patients’ Rights](https://www.nilc.org/resources/healthcare-provider-and-patients-rights-imm-enf/)
- **Nonprofits and Small Business Owners** [Frequently Asked Questions](https://publiccounsel.org/publications/faqs-on-nonprofit-and-small-business-rights-with-respect-to-immigration-enforcement-ice/) on Nonprofit and Small Business Rights with Respect to Immigration Enforcement (ICE).
- **Restaurants** [These Are Your Rights](https://lataco.com/ice-rights-restaurant) if a restaurant gets raided by ICE, for both customers and owners.
