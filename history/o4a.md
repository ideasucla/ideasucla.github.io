# Opportunity for All Campaign
The Opportunity for All campaign (Assembly Bill 713) aims to correct the current interpretation of the law, allowing undocumented students to be hired in all California state schools, including the University of California campuses, California State Universities, and California Community Colleges. You learn more about the history of this campaign [here](https://www.weareijac.org/background-and-campaign-history).

# Immigrant Justic in Action Coalition
>The Immigrant Justice in Action Coalition (IJAC) is a statewide, immigrant-led organization in California advancing intersectional immigrant justice. Our work includes advocacy, organizing, legal strategies, leadership development, and community care rooted in equity, solidarity, and self-determination. IJAC operates as a grassroots, member-driven coalition guided by undocumented and mixed-status leadership.

IJAC is committed to uplifting undocumented communities, allies, and aligned organizations by amplifying voices, sharing undocumented narratives, and cultivating transformative relationships — all in pursuit of abolition, justice, and love.

[See more](https://www.weareijac.org/) and follow IJEC on [Instagram!](https://www.instagram.com/immigrantjusticeca?utm_source=ig_web_button_share_sheet&igsh=ZDNlZDc0MzIxNw==)
