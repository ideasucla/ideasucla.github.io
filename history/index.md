# History of IDEAS at UCLA
IDEAS (Improving Dreams, Equality, Access, and Success) is a school organization that provides undocumented students with resources that help them navigate the higher education system, with the first co-chairs elected in the 2004-5 academic school year. IDEAS is built around four cour pillars: membership, activism, fundraising, and community service. Each pillar is portrayed through the eagerness to be creative, supportive, and intuitive for those who rely on IDEAS’s available resources, whether it is in workshops or while mentoring students and parents in need of resourceful information.
