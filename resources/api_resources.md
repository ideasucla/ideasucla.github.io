# APDA/API-Specific Resources
Resources tailored to the Asian Pacific Islander Desi American communities. This list includes organizations focused on protecting and uplifting APDA/API folks and resources in APDA/API languages.

- Multi-lingual [Red cards](https://www.ilrc.org/redcards).
- Multi-lingual Know your Rights [video](https://www.wehaverights.us/).
- [Red cards](https://ajsocal.org/immigration/#toolkit:~:text=YOUR%20RIGHTS%20VIDEOS-,RED,-CARD) in English, Chinese, Japanese, Khmer, Korean, and Vietnamese. 
- [Crowdsource](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1QJ_pDXZ0_nfcxIKKA5GbdOYr53uuyZQ1HDVYxWoEHS0/htmlview?gid=0#gid=0) community compilation of resources labeled by applicable state and language.
- National Korean American Service and Education Consortium
    - [Know Your Rights](https://nakasec.org/resources/know-your-rights/) information in English and Korean.
    - Includes Know Your Rights mobile app.
- Asian Americans Advancing Justice Southern California: [Immigration Resources](https://ajsocal.org/immigration/) with multiple toolkits and guides in multiple languages.
- AFT Immigrant and Refugee Children:  Educator and School Support Guide in [English](https://www.aft.org/sites/default/files/media/2017/im_uac-educators-guide_2017.pdf) and [Korena](https://www.aft.org/sites/default/files/media/documents/2023/im_uac-educators-guide-Korean_2019.pdf).
- Koreatown Immigrant Workers Alliance (KIWA): Immigrant community [outreach and education](https://www.kiwa.org/outreach-and-education).
- Know Your Righths [Cards](https://linktr.ee/RedCardsMoreLanguages) in 110+ languages.
